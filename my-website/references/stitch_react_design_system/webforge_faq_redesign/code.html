FAQ

Common questions answered clearly.

How long does a typical project take?

expand_more

Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope. A standard high-performance conversion landing page typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a comprehensive end-to-end digital system or multi-page platform usually requires 8-12 weeks of meticulous craftsmanship from initial discovery to final hand-off.

Do you offer post-launch support?

expand_more

Absolutely. We view our deployments as long-term partnerships. Every Webforge project includes a 30-day "hyper-care" monitoring phase. Beyond that, we offer tiered "Forge Maintenance" packages that cover security updates, performance optimization, and iterative feature development to ensure your system remains at peak performance.

What is your pricing structure?

expand_more

We operate on a value-based, fixed-price model for most projects to provide absolute clarity and budget security. For specific high-growth ventures or ongoing R&D, we offer retainer-based arrangements. Each quote is bespoke, handcrafted to reflect the specific technical requirements and business objectives of your studio forge.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

expand_more

Precision integration is our specialty. We excel at taking established brand DNA and evolving it into a high-performance digital environment. Our "Blueprint" process ensures that every color, font, and interaction aligns perfectly with your existing identity while introducing technical improvements for modern web standards.

What makes Webforge different from a standard agency?

expand_more

The difference lies in our "Forge" philosophy. Most agencies use standardized templates; we build handcrafted systems. We prioritize technical performance, conversion architecture, and editorial-grade design over generic trends. You aren't just getting a website; you are getting a precision-engineered tool designed to yield measurable business outcomes.
