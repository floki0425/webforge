# Digital Forge Design System Export

## Brand Philosophy
Webforge is a premium digital studio specializing in "laboratory-grade" digital ecosystems. The design language emphasizes precision, performance, and craftsmanship.

## Color Palette
- **Obsidian Black**: `#080808` (Primary Background / High Contrast)
- **Warm Ivory**: `#F7F3EC` (Secondary Background / Paper texture feel)
- **Champagne Gold**: `#C8A96A` (Primary Accents / Interactive states)
- **Burnished Copper**: `#B76E42` (Secondary Accents / Detail highlights)
- **Charcoal Text**: `#1F2933` (Primary Body)
- **Muted Slate**: `#667085` (Secondary Body / Captions)
- **Soft Stone**: `#E7E1D6` (Borders / Dividers)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Hanken Grotesk
- **Headings**: Bold, editorial, tight letter-spacing.
- **Body**: Modern, readable, generous line-height (1.6-1.8).

## Components
- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped, weighted shadows, champagne hover transitions.
- **Cards**: Layered with soft shadows, subtle stone borders, ivory or midnight backgrounds.
- **Navigation**: Minimalist, sticky, backdrop-blur support.

## Motion Guidelines
- **Easing**: `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`
- **Entrance Duration**: 500ms
- **Interaction Duration**: 200ms
- **Stagger Delay**: 80ms
